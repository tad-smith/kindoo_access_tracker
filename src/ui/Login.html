G Sign in with Google

First-time users will see a one-time Google prompt asking to share your email address with this app — that's expected. Click Allow; you'll land back here automatically.

Welcome to Kindoo Access Tracker

First time signing in?

  1. Click Got it, sign me in below.
  2. You’ll see a screen asking you to Review Permissions. Click that button.
  3. Next, Google will show what Kindoo needs (just your email address). Click Continue.
  4. Finally, you’ll see a Kindoo confirmation page. Click Continue one more time.
  5. After that, the app will load and you won’t need to repeat these steps.

About the “Unverified” warning

Step 2 will display an “(Unverified)” warning. This is Google’s way of saying Kindoo Access Tracker isn’t a publicly-listed app. It’s an internal tool built and maintained for the stake. The only data Kindoo reads from Google is your email address — used to identify you and determine which ward you serve.
