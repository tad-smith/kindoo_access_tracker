Not authorized

The email doesn't have a role in this app.

Possible causes:

  • You were recently called to a bishopric or the stake presidency. The importer runs weekly, so your access will appear after the next import.
  • Your sign-in email differs from the address recorded for your calling. Gmail dot and +suffix variants are matched automatically; other variations need a Kindoo Manager to update the record.
  • You're not a Kindoo Manager, bishopric member, or stake-presidency member.

Contact a Kindoo Manager if you need access sooner.
